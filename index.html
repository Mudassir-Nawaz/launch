Meet Dalia

I’ve been where you are—building something I believed in and trying to market it without getting lost in jargon or overcomplicated strategies. That’s why I created Launch with Dalia, to make marketing feel doable, strategic, and actually fun.

How I Got Here

I launched my first online business when people still used print ads. You could call it old-school; I call it foundational.

I started in the early 2010s, building Moms of Fredericksburg before digital marketing was even cool. I created content, led campaigns, built partnerships, and connected small businesses with real families who needed them.

Since then, I’ve helped franchises, startups, insurance agencies, and brick-and-mortar businesses craft brands with clarity, strategy, and guts. Now I use that experience to help you move from chaos to clarity with a plan that works.

What Drives Me

Marketing is more than making noise — it’s about making sense, and making sales.

I believe in cutting through the noise with marketing that feels human. My mission is to empower entrepreneurs to share their vision in a way that resonates and gets results.
